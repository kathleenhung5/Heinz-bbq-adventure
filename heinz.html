Carolina Mustard Style Bbq Sauce

From Columbia to Charleston, South Carolina’s “mustard belt” is where tangy mustard-based barbecue sauce reigns supreme. That’s why Heinz teamed up with South Carolina native Bryan Furman, owner and head pitmaster of the award-winning B’s Cracklin BBQ. From slow-cooked pulled pork to ribs on the grill, this golden sauce delivers the right balance of sweetness and spice that will redefine swine.
